Cogolin manufactory

Founded at the beginning of the 20th century, in the small town of Cogolin, near Saint-Tropez, in the south of France, the Manufacture de Cogolin weaves carpets known all over the world. It is one of the last manufacturers to defend a human heritage, a typically French manufacture and a universe before any craft. The finished products, handmade and made to measure, decorate the floors of the most beautiful homes in the world.

Ozone light

Ozone, the light that makes you dream. Workshop founded in 2000, Ozone highlights spaces and designs lighting fixtures designed and assembled in his Paris studio. The need for finishes, the very architectural and refined forms of the products lead the greatest interior designers to work with Ozone. "Playing with light is an art: it must carve the space, integrate into the architecture so as to be absent while being present" Etienne Gounot, founder.

4BI & Associés

One of the strengths of this interior design firm lies in the subtle, elegant, refined but warm integration of French culture for each of its achievements around the world. In 2002, the Cartier house entrusted him with the new concept and the development of its boutiques in the world. Another remarkable achievement, Château Latour in Pauillac (owner François Pinault). In Paris, the Plaza Athénée hotel (2014). Its solidly established reputation extends into Europe, such as the Dorchester Hotel (2014) and the Four Seasons Hotel in London (2015), and increasingly in Asia, Japan and especially China with two hotels in Chengdu (2013).

Elliott Barnes Interiors

Architecte franco-américain, Elliott Barnes écrit des histoires architecturales et décoratives autour de la lumière, des matériaux et des couleurs, afin d’en extraire la juste émotion. En France, comme à l’international, les réalisations Elliott Barnes respectent les envies de ses clients et valorisent leurs espaces, tout en proposant une approche éclectique du luxe, formellement épurée et toujours innovante.

Art de Vivre is born of a passion between 4 partners of different cultural horizons whose common denominator is the love of beauty and beautiful interior. An Art of Living that manifests itself in the art of living and integrating refined furniture into elegant and harmonious decorations. Every place, every owner has his story; our role is to take it into account at the beginning of the project.
